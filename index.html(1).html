SMS Messaging & Twilio

Achieve uses Twilio as its SMS and voice infrastructure provider to power missed call text-back and outreach messaging services. By engaging our SMS automation services, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

How It Works

When a customer calls a business phone number enrolled in our missed call text-back service and the call goes unanswered, our system automatically sends a single SMS message to the caller's phone number. This message is sent on behalf of the business client and may be AI-generated.

Example automated message:

"Hey! Sorry we missed your call — we'd love to help. What can we do for you? Reply any time and we'll get back to you shortly."

Message Frequency

For missed call text-back services, end-users (callers) will receive a maximum of one (1) automated SMS per missed call event. Message frequency may vary for other outreach campaigns based on the specific service configuration agreed upon with the client.

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply to end-users receiving SMS messages through our system. These rates are determined by the end-user's mobile carrier and are outside of Achieve's control.

Twilio Compliance

All SMS messaging conducted through Achieve's services is subject to Twilio's Acceptable Use Policy and Messaging Policy. Clients agree not to use Achieve's services in any manner that would violate Twilio's terms of service or applicable telecommunications laws including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and Canadian Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

Consent to Receive Messages

Achieve's missed call text-back service operates on the basis of implied consent. By initiating a phone call to a business number enrolled in our service, the caller implicitly consents to receive a single automated SMS response acknowledging their missed call.

Implied consent is established through the end-user's own voluntary action of placing a phone call to the business. The automated text-back is a direct response to that call attempt, not unsolicited marketing communication.

Business Client Responsibility

Business clients who engage Achieve's outreach automation or marketing SMS services are solely responsible for ensuring they have obtained proper express written consent from recipients prior to sending any marketing messages. Achieve provides the technical infrastructure only — the client is the message sender of record and bears full legal responsibility for consent compliance.

No Sharing of Consent Data

SMS opt-in data and consent records will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This information is used solely to operate the agreed-upon service.

05

Opt-Out Rights

All end-users who receive SMS messages through Achieve's systems have the right to opt out at any time. To stop receiving messages, end-users may reply with any of the following keywords:

  • STOP
  • CANCEL
  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • QUIT
  • END

Upon receiving an opt-out request, the system will send one final confirmation message and immediately cease sending further messages to that number. Achieve's systems automatically honor opt-out requests in compliance with TCPA and CASL requirements.

To opt back in after opting out, end-users may reply START or UNSTOP.

For help at any time, end-users may reply HELP to receive support contact information.
